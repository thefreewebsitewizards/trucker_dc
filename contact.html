Frequently Asked Questions

What types of freight do you haul?

We specialize in general freight, dry van loads, and standard commercial deliveries across the 48 contiguous states. Our equipment is well-maintained and suitable for most standard freight requirements.

How do you calculate freight rates?

Rates are calculated based on distance, cargo type, weight, delivery timeframe, and current market conditions. We provide competitive pricing and transparent quotes for all services.

Do you offer expedited shipping?

Yes, we offer expedited and time-critical freight services. Our 24/7 dispatch ensures we can accommodate urgent delivery requirements when needed.

How can I track my shipment?

We provide real-time tracking updates via phone, email, or text. You'll receive regular status updates throughout the delivery process.

What about content collaborations?

We're open to brand partnerships, sponsored content, and collaborations that align with our trucking and lifestyle content. Reach out with your ideas!
