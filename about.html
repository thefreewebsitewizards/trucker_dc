About Clopton National LLC

Clopton National LLC was founded by DeMarea & Paige Clopton with a vision to revolutionize the trucking industry through professionalism, reliability, and family values. We specialize in long-haul refrigerated freight, logistics solutions, and driver growth.

Rooted in professionalism and powered by experience, our company has built a reputation for excellence in freight transportation. We understand that every load carries more than just cargo—it carries trust, responsibility, and the promise of delivery.

Our Core Values

  • Safety First - Every mile, every load
  • Family Values - Treating drivers like family
  • Professional Excellence - Setting industry standards
  • Reliability - On-time, every time

About Trucker DC

DeMarea 'Trucker DC' Clopton is a long-haul trucker, YouTube creator, and lifestyle storyteller who has captured the hearts of over 42,600 subscribers and generated more than 1 million views across his content platforms.

Through his authentic storytelling, Trucker DC shares the real journey of life on the road—the grind, the glory, and everything in between. His content provides an unfiltered look into the trucking lifestyle, from cross-country hauls to the daily challenges and triumphs of professional driving.
